    FAQs

    Q. How many flavors are available?

    A- Penny Juice concentrates are available in 15 delicious flavors including 7 color-free.

    Q. Where is Penny Juice manufactured?

    A- All our juice concentrates are made in Canada from domstic and imported ingredients.

    Q. Can I do a onetime order or do I need a contract?

    A- No contract is required to order PennyJuice.

    Q. How much does shipping cost?

    A- We offer free nationwide shipping on all orders.

    Q. What does concentrate mean?

    A- concentrate juice is fruit juice with some of the water removed. Because water has been removed, the juice concentrate can be stored in a much smaller container than typical bottled juice.

    Q. Is Penny Juice gluten-free?

    A- We cannot guarantee that out fruit juice concentrates have been prepared in a gluten-free facility.

    Q. How many calories are in a glass of Penny Juice?

    A- The reconstituted juice has 80 calories in a 4 oz serving size. It has zero fat, is low in sodium and carbohydrates. It is a source of potassium and provides 100% of the daily requirement for vitamin C.
